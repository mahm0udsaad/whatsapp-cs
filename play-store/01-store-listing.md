### Full description (≤ 4000 chars)
```
Nehgz Bot is a WhatsApp customer-service control panel built for restaurant managers and their teams. It combines a unified inbox, marketing campaigns, and live team performance so you can reply to customers faster and grow revenue from one place.

✦ Smart inbox
- See every WhatsApp conversation in a single, sortable list.
- Filter by Unassigned, Mine, Bot-handled, or Archived.
- Reassign chats to the right teammate with one tap.
- Tag conversations with custom labels for status or priority.

✦ Approvals & escalations
- Get a push notification the moment the AI bot asks for your approval.
- Review the extracted customer intent and edit the reply before it sends.
- Pull the full conversation history in one screen.

✦ WhatsApp marketing campaigns
- Send free-text or Meta-approved template campaigns.
- Track sent / delivered / read counters in real time.
- Target a customer segment or your whole list.

✦ Team management
- Add staff, assign roles (manager, supervisor, agent), control permissions.
- See team performance live: response time, conversations closed, CSAT.
- Manage shifts and see who's online right now.

✦ Customer directory
- Every customer with their full chat history and private labels.
- Search and filter by name, phone or label in seconds.

✦ WhatsApp health monitoring
- Live indicator of your number's Meta quality rating.
- Alerts before quality drops and your messages stop reaching customers.

✦ Arabic-first
- Native Arabic UI, RTL layout, locale-aware dates.
- Push notifications for urgent messages and approval requests.

Nehgz Bot connects securely to your restaurant's WhatsApp Business Platform account — no personal numbers are used. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and is never shared with third parties for advertising.

This is a B2B tool: each manager needs an account on the Nehgz Bot platform and a WhatsApp Business number linked to your restaurant. Sign-up & support: support@nehgzbot.com
```
